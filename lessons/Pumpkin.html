Paperplate Pumpkin

Amount

1
Items needed:
  • 1 orange Paper Plates
  • glue
  • Black and Green Construction Paper
  • scissors

Instructions:

  1. First, cut the green construction paper into a stem shape, then glue to the back of the paper plate.
  2. Next, cut the black construction paper into a eye, nose, and mouth shape. Glue these items to the center of the paper plate.
